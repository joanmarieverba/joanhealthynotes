Need a food diary? The 12-week (Universal Food Diary) or 6-week (My Food Diary) diet journal is adaptable to almost any diet. Whatever you’re tracking—calories, carbs, fat, fiber, sodium, or other units—this food tracker has a place for it. You can also record your activity or mood. At the beginning of every week, there’s a space to record your goals for the week, and any notes. There’s also a space for you to plan fun activities for the week. (Yes, you need to take time to have fun!). More than one research study has shown that keeping track of what you eat can be helpful. So track your food—the results may astound you!
